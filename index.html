The Basics of Photography

Introduction

Photography is the art, application, and practice of creating images by capturing light. Photographs are taken using cameras, this is done as the camera record the light entering the camera.

With advancements in technology, photography has become accessible to everyone, enabling people to capture moments and share their perspectives with others.

Mobile Devices puts the power of search engines, applicatons, and cameras right at our fingetips, allowing us to post, save, and share our memories with eachother at the press of a button.

Types of Photography

There are many types of photography, each requiring different techniques and skills. Some popular types include portrait, landscape, street, and wildlife photography. National geographic showcases exceptional photography in the field of nature.

Each genre has its own unique approach, from capturing the natural world to focusing on human expressions and emotionsw making it a very interesting choise for many

Basic Photography Techniques

Photography involves understanding a few basic techniques, such as controlling exposure, focusing, and composing the shot. Exposure settings include aperture, shutter speed, and ISO.

With practice, photographers learn to balance these settings to create clear and compelling images in a variety of lighting conditions.
